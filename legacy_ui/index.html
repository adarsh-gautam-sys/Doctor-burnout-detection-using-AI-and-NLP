Burnout Begins Before the Breaking Point.

An AI and NLP pipeline that reads a doctor's handwriting to detect burnout risk — automatically, before errors happen.

The Problem

A crisis hiding in plain sight.

40–60%

Physicians worldwide report symptoms of burnout — emotional exhaustion, depersonalization, or reduced personal accomplishment.

Delayed Detection Problem

Existing burnout detection relies on surveys and self-reporting, which are delayed and subjective. By the time burnout is identified, it often already impacts clinical performance.

0

Prescription images analyzed across real and synthetic document sources.

"Burnt-out doctors write differently. Shorter sentences. Shakier lines. More abbreviations. Less detail. This is a signal — and it's been invisible. Until now."

Handwriting Degradation

Motor control degrades under chronic stress. Stroke pressure drops, letter consistency erodes, and spatial regularity declines — measurable changes that an OCR pipeline can quantify.

Linguistic Shortcuts

Burnt-out physicians use more abbreviations, shorter sentences, and fewer diagnostic qualifiers. NLP analysis detects these patterns as a ratio change over time.

Negative Sentiment Drift

Clinical notes shift in tone — less patient-centered language, more terseness, fewer empathetic markers. Sentiment analysis captures this gradual drift before it becomes a crisis.

Scale the Dataset

200 training samples are insufficient for a 333.9M parameter model. Target: 10,000+ annotated medical document images across all three document types and multiple handwriting styles.

The FastAPI + Docker stack is production-ready. Partner with a teaching hospital to deploy the pipeline on real prescriptions and validate burnout signal detection in a clinical setting.

Longitudinal Tracking

Track per-doctor handwriting and linguistic patterns over weeks and months. Detect drift toward burnout indicators before they manifest as diagnostic errors or patient complaints.
